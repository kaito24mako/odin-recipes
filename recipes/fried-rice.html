Tom Yum Fried Rice


Description

This is my unique recipe of Tom Yum Fried Rice. Please follow the recipe carefully!

Ingredients

For one portion:

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 3 tablespoons of Tom Yum paste
  • 150g of chicken thighs
  • 1 carrot
  • 3 teaspoons of sesame oil
  • 1 large-sized frying pan
  • Virgin oil

Steps

Follow these steps carefully!

  1. Cut the carrot into long, thin slices. Make sure the slices are thin enough so the carrots would not take too long to cook.
  2. Slice the cabbage into medium slices. It should be a similar length to the carrots, but around 3cm wide.
  3. Heat up the frying pan. Wait until you can feel the heat as you hover your hand above the pan.
  4. Add 3 teaspoons of sesame oil into the heating pan. Wait until you can see the heat rise up.
  5. Add the carrots and chicken thighs into the pan. Cook until the chicken thighs become white, and the carrots are soft.
  6. Add the rice into the frying pan. Cook for 30 seconds, then add the 3 tablespoons of Tom Yum paste. Cook and stir thoroughly to make sure no chunks of Tom Yum paste can be seen.
  7. Plate the food beautifully. You have finished!
