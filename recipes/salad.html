Simple Green Salad

Description

This is a quick recipe on how to make a simple, healthy green salad!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of rocket greens
  • 1 pack of leaf salad
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tablespoons of sesame dressing

Steps

Follow these steps carefully:

  1. Wash all of the vegetables first!
  2. Cut the cucumber into half slices, 1cm wide.
  3. Cut the onion into long, thin slices.
  4. Mix all the vegetables together in a large bowl.
  5. Pour 3 tablespoons of sesame dressing over the salad.
  6. Enjoy!
