Instant Cup Noodles (Nissin)

Description

Here is a quick recipe guide on how to make Nissin cup noodles. I am recommending Nissin to you as they are, by far and in large,, the best cup noodle brand in the world.

No one can tell you otherwise.

Ingredients

  • 1 instant Nissin noodle cup
  • 1 packet of paste or powder (the flavour can be whatever is in the cup)
  • 250ml of boiling water

Steps

Follow these steps carefully:

  1. Boil water to full heat.
  2. Add the sauce or powder into the instant noodle cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water into the instant noodle cup, close the lid, and leave it for three minutes.
  4. After the three minutes, stir the noodles thoroughly until all the paste or powder has dissolved and the noodles are seperated from one another.
  5. You have successfully made your Nissin Noodle Cup!
